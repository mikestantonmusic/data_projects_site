Data Scientist

I specialize in answering fundamental questions from large and complex data sets

As deep learning becomes more and more prevalent, and as data becomes larger and more complex, there is a growing need to understand the answers to some of the most basic questions about our data. Often it is not enough to build a model with excellent performance. Knowing what features are the most important, where problems are with the data, and identifying general trends and patterns can help us to make better decisions more efficiently. This kind of explainability can prevent failures, help tune the model, improve confidence, and in some cases even save resources by circumventing the need for machine learning altogether.
